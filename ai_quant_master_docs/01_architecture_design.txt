AI Trading Architecture Guide
Overview
Institutional grade AI trading systems separate intelligence, strategy, risk, and execution layers to ensure
stability and reproducibility.

Architecture
Data Layer ↓ Feature Engineering ↓ AI Market Intelligence (regime detection / sentiment) ↓ Strategy
Engine (trend / mean reversion / arbitrage) ↓ Risk Engine (position + portfolio control) ↓ Execution Engine
(order router / exchange API) ↓ Monitoring Dashboard (PnL / metrics / alerts)

Key Principles
Modular architecture, deterministic risk control, reproducible backtests, and robust order execution.
